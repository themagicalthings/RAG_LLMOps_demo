   
 
   
 
Senior Platform Engineer 
Why this Role Matters: 
At Greenbox Capital, we help small businesses thrive by giving them fast, accessible funding. As 
a Senior Platform Engineer, you’ll play a key role in modernizing our origination ecosystem by 
building scalable, high-performance platform services that power critical business workflows. 
You’ll drive results in platform transformation, API development, and system integration, and 
your work will directly support our growth, operational efficiency, and ability to innovate. 
This role reports to VP of Tech and operates as an individual contributor.  
What Success Looks Like:  
Here’s how your time might break down (actual time can shift depending on business needs): 
• Platform Modernization & Legacy Replacement – Reducing reliance on legacy 
systems through scalable service development 
• API & Integration Delivery – Building secure, reliable APIs and supporting 
internal/external integrations 
• Platform Reliability & Engineering Excellence – Driving code quality, observability, 
and scalable architecture 
How you’ll be measured:  
Platform Modernization & Architecture Execution 
• Deliver scalable services and components that successfully replace legacy platform 
functionality 
 
API & Integration Engineering 
• Design and implement secure, maintainable APIs and integrations that support 
internal systems and third-party platforms 
 
Cross-Functional Collaboration & Technical Leadership 
• Partner effectively with engineering, data, and product teams while influencing 
architecture, standards, and delivery practices 
You’re a Strong Fit if You:  
• Demonstrate the ability to design and build scalable backend systems in complex, 
data-intensive environments 
• Have a strong ownership mindset and can operate autonomously in ambiguous 
technical situations 
• Collaborate effectively across engineering, product, and data teams in a distributed 
environment 
   
 
   
 
• Communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical stakeholders 
• Bring a pragmatic approach to building vs. buying solutions and can justify trade-offs 
• Are naturally curious, adaptable, and thrive in a fast-moving, evolving organization 
like Greenbox 
What You’ve Done Before:  
• Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent 
experience) 
• 7+ years of professional software engineering experience 
• 5+ years building scalable backend services and APIs using Python 
• Proven experience contributing to or leading platform modernization or migration 
initiatives 
• Experience working in cloud-native environments (preferably Azure) 
• Experience designing and operating production-grade REST APIs 
• Background in regulated industries such as FinTech, Banking, or Healthcare is a plus 
Tools and Expertise You’ll Bring:  
• Advanced Python (service development, testing, packaging, maintainable design) 
• Databricks, Spark, and data platform ecosystems (required) 
• REST API design, authentication, versioning, and error handling best practices 
• Frameworks such as FastAPI or Flask 
• Azure cloud services (compute, storage, networking, identity, monitoring, API 
management) 
• CI/CD pipelines, version control, and tooling (Azure DevOps, GitHub Actions) 
• DevOps fundamentals including deployment pipelines and secrets management 
• Experience with relational and NoSQL databases, event-driven architectures, and 
asynchronous processing 
• Nice-to-have: Vue.js or modern frontend frameworks, Streamlit, Terraform/Bicep, 
AI-enabled application patterns, and familiarity with .NET/C# 
What to Expect from Our Interview Process 
We believe in a respectful, efficient, and transparent hiring experience. Here’s what you can 
expect: 
 
Step 1: Initial Phone Screen (30 minutes) 
A brief conversation with a recruiter to learn more about your background, interests, and 
alignment with the role. 
Step 2: Hiring Manager Interview (1 hour) 
A deeper discussion about the role, your relevant experience, and how you’d contribute to the 
team. 
   
 
   
 
Step 3: Role-Specific Assessment or Panel Interview (1 hour) 
Depending on the role, this may include a take-home assignment, technical interview, or live 
case study with team members. 
Step 4: Final Interview or Leadership Chat (1 hour) 
A final conversation with senior leadership or cross-functional team members to ensure 
alignment with our mission and values. 
Step 5: Offer & Background Check 
If it’s a mutual fit, we’ll move forward with background check and present a competitive offer.
